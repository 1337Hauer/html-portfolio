Chris's favorite movies in no particular order

My top 3 favorite movies of all time

Everything Everywhere All at Once

Really fun movie that is both artistic and profound in the way it depicts life and reality.

American History X

A powerful and dramatic story about a family that is consumed by prejudice and sheds light on why it's not worth it to harbor animosity toward others.

The Bourne Identity

An action movie about a man that can't remember who is even though he has some mad skills.
